# ⚡ Pure Fit: AI Performance Coach (Gemini 2.5 Edition)

**Pure Fit** is a high-performance fitness and nutrition application built with Python and **Google's Gemini 2.5 Flash**. It is specifically engineered for the student community to provide personalized health strategies that respect budget constraints, hostel living conditions, and academic schedules.



## 🌟 Key Features

* **Dynamic Setup:** Tailors all AI outputs based on your specific student profile, including goals and current fitness level.
* **AI Workout Architect:** Uses **Gemini 2.5** to build custom training splits for any environment—from a full gym to a small hostel room.
* **Student-Budget Nutrition:** Generates 7-day meal plans focused on affordable, locally available Indian ingredients (e.g., Dal, Paneer, Soya).
* **Vision-Based Macro Analysis:** Upload meal photos for instant AI identification and nutritional estimation (Calories, Protein, Carbs, Fats).
* **Recovery & Mental Clarity:** Provides specialized protocols to manage physical soreness alongside academic stress.
* **Health Insight:** Provide answers to genreal health related issues
---

## 🛠️ Technology Stack

* **AI Engine:** [Google Gemini 2.5 Flash](https://aistudio.google.com/) 
* **Frontend:** Streamlit
* **Image Handling:** PIL (Pillow)
